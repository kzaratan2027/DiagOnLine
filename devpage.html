THE MUGGLE WAY TO MAGIC!


The mass extinction of the Homo sapien magnificus, commonly known as witches and wizards, was caused by the Second Wizarding War led by two propagators: Harry Potter and Lord Voldemort. Non-magic folk like us were called “muggles” in their world, whom which Potter adored being raised in a non-magical household with a late mother who was a “muggleborn” witch. Voldemort, however, despised these muggles and sought to eliminate all muggleborns in the wizarding world and eventually, even non-magic folk uninvolved in their world. As they cast their final death spell known as “Avada Kedavra” concurrently, their merged magic as the two of the greatest and most powerful wizards of all time, spread the curse amongst all witches and wizards globally. At an instant, all magic folk ceased to exist, and the “Obliviate” spell cast on us, muggles, intended to hide the existence of magic, also wore away at once. Soon came the phenomenon “The Great Awakening of 2024” as all muggles around the world simultaneously become aware of the former magic that was once there, hidden from their reach. The remnants of these two historical events are sold on this website as magical artifacts.
